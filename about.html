About Us

Vision

To be a financially viable, independent community bank that is committed to improving the quality of life of the communities we serve. To earn the loyalty of employees, customers and the community by operating with integrity and fairness at all times. To demonstrate behavior that totally focuses on the customer and recognize they are the reason we are here. To have employees who are empowered to build long-term relationships with our customers.

Values

  • Integrity & Ethics
  • Commitment
  • Leadership
  • Positive legacy
  • Mutual respect

Our mission is to creates a positive economic impact in our communities, and creates positive personal and professional opportunities for our employees.
